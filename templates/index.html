About Helping Hands

Helping Hands is a comprehensive retail staff shift management and volunteer system designed to streamline workforce coordination for retail businesses.

Our platform enables managers to efficiently create and manage shifts, while empowering staff members to volunteer for shifts that fit their schedules. With real-time notifications, comprehensive reporting, and role-based access control, Helping Hands transforms the way retail teams collaborate.

  • Efficient shift scheduling
  • Real-time volunteer management
  • Instant notifications
  • Comprehensive reporting

Key Features

Shift Management

Create, update, and manage shifts with ease. Set specific time slots, assign roles, and track volunteer capacity.

Volunteer System

Staff can volunteer for available shifts, and managers can approve or reject applications with one click.

Smart Notifications

Stay updated with real-time notifications for shift approvals, rejections, and important updates.

Analytics & Reports

Generate comprehensive reports, export data to CSV, and gain insights into shift patterns.

Role-Based Access

Secure authentication with role-based permissions for Admin, Manager, and Staff users.

Audit Logs

Track all user activities with comprehensive audit logging for security and compliance.
